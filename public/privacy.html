Privacy Policy & Data Safety

Last Updated: November 21, 2024

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We take your privacy seriously. This survey collects information about your health experiences, relationship dynamics, and product preferences. Here's exactly how we protect your data.

Information We Collect

We collect:

  • Demographic information (age group, relationship status)
  • Health tracking behaviors and preferences
  • Experiences with health management and partner communication
  • Product feature preferences and willingness to pay
  • Optional open-ended responses about your experiences
  • Optional contact information (only if you consent to follow-up)

We DO NOT collect:

  • Your name (unless you provide it voluntarily)
  • Precise location data
  • Payment information
  • Health records or medical history
  • Any identifying information unless you explicitly provide it

How We Use Your Information

Your responses are used to:

  • Identify gaps in the women's health technology market
  • Understand common challenges women face
  • Develop product features that address real needs
  • Determine target audiences and pricing strategies
  • Generate anonymized insights and statistics

Your data is NEVER:

  • Sold to third parties
  • Shared with advertisers
  • Used to identify you personally
  • Shared publicly in identifiable form

How We Protect Your Data

Security measures:

  • Data stored in secure, encrypted Firebase database
  • Access restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Industry-standard encryption protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Automatic data backup and recovery systems

Data retention:

  • Survey responses stored indefinitely for research purposes
  • All data stored in anonymized format
  • You can request deletion of your specific response by contacting us

Anonymity Options

By default, your survey is completely anonymous:

  • No personal identifiers required
  • Responses cannot be traced back to you
  • We cannot identify who submitted which responses

If you choose to provide contact information:

  • This is optional and only at the end of the survey
  • Used solely for potential follow-up interviews
  • Stored separately from your survey responses
  • You can request removal at any time

Who Has Access to Your Data

Internal use:

  • Product development team (to analyze gaps and build features)
  • Researchers (to generate insights and recommendations)
  • All access is logged and monitored

External sharing:

  • Aggregated, anonymized statistics may be shared publicly (e.g., "60% of respondents experience X")
  • Individual responses are NEVER shared
  • No third-party marketing or advertising use
  • No sale of data to external companies

AI Processing:

  • We use Google Gemini AI to analyze survey responses
  • Data is sent securely to Gemini API for pattern analysis
  • Gemini processes data but does not store it permanently
  • All AI analysis follows Google's privacy and security standards

Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your survey response (contact us with approximate submission date/time)
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for follow-up contact
  • Ask questions about how your data is used
  • Request a copy of aggregated insights (if applicable)

How to exercise your rights:
Email us at privacy@theshetalks.club with your request.

Cookies and Browser Storage

We use:

  • Browser localStorage to auto-save your survey progress (so you don't lose answers if you close the browser)
  • No tracking cookies
  • No advertising cookies
  • No analytics cookies

Local storage is:

  • Stored only on your device
  • Automatically cleared after survey submission
  • Not accessible to third parties

Age Requirement

This survey is intended for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you are under 18, please do not complete this survey.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be communicated if you provided contact information.

Questions or Concerns?

Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, contact us:

Email: privacy@theshetalks.club
Response time: Within 48 hours

Legal Basis & Compliance

  • This survey complies with data protection regulations
  • Data processing is based on your voluntary consent
  • You may withdraw consent at any time
  • Data is processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently
